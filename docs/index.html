What is MentalBridge?

MentalBridge is a mental health first-aid companion that runs entirely on your device using Google's Gemma 4 model via Ollama. It supports Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, Kannada and English — automatically detecting your language. Every conversation stays on your device forever.

It was built for the 197 million Indians who suffer in silence — not because they don't want help, but because saying the words out loud feels impossible. MentalBridge gives them a safe first conversation.

💬

Warm AI Chat

Gemma 4 responds warmly in your language. Short sentences. One gentle question. No clinical jargon.

📵

100% Offline

Everything runs on your device via Ollama. Turn off WiFi — it still works. Your words never leave your laptop.

How to Run It

MentalBridge runs locally on your laptop. Setup takes about 5 minutes.

1

Install Ollama

Download and install Ollama from ollama.com — available for Mac, Windows and Linux.

2

Download Gemma 4

Run this command once to download the Gemma 4 E2B model (7.2GB):

ollama pull gemma4:e2b
3

Download MentalBridge

Download the ZIP file above, unzip it, and double-click START.sh

bash START.sh
4

Open in browser

MentalBridge opens automatically at:

http://localhost:8080
